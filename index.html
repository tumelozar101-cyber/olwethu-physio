About the practice

Your Dedicated
Physiotherapist

OLPhysio is a patient-centred physiotherapy practice based in Welkom, Bloemfontein, providing high-quality care for individuals at every stage of their recovery journey.

With a BSc in Physiotherapy from the University of KwaZulu-Natal (UKZN), our practice is built on clinical excellence and genuine care for every patient's long-term wellbeing.

Whether you're recovering from a sports injury, managing chronic pain, or navigating post-operative rehabilitation — we're here to guide you every step of the way with an evidence-based, personalised approach.
